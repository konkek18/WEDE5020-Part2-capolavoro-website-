About Us

Founded by Konke Khoza, Capolavoro is more than a makeup studio-it's a celebration of individiuality. The name, meaning "masterpiece" in Italian, reflects our core belief: every person is a unique work of art.

With over 3 years of professional experience, we specialize in bridal, editorial, and special event makeup. Our mission is to enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident and radiant.

We believe that makeup is a tool for self-expression, and our goal is to help you reveal masterpiece that you already are.
